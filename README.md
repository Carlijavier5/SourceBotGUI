# SourceBotGUI

A GUI wrapper for SourceBot with custom result rankings, deployable at your convenience.

<i>Built with Unity 6.0.4 (Supported)</i>

Build Link: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1hQp1MVpjXdu0fcvmiE3SLfVvthvtFP3d/view?usp=sharing

# User-End Instructions:

You may unzip SoureBotGUI_Final.zip
In the extracted folder, you may run SourceBotGUI.exe to launch the app. You will be greeted by a "Made with Unity" screen, followed by the following welcome screen:

<img width="702" height="399" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/adfbda95-b116-4ca0-96c6-ecdbe28d6c18" />

You may input a query here, which you may further customize by selecting the ranking algorithm you wish to run on your results, and/or filter your results by language.

After submitting a query and awaiting a few transitions, your app may look like this:

<img width="686" height="374" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3e525e08-5ccd-48f2-b005-6f7e4c4fe9dc" />

If more than 20 matches are found, they will be paged, and you may cycle through said result pages through the arrows at the bottom right;
Every link, be it in the Repository section or the Code Snippet section, is interactable, and will take you to the corresponding piece of code on GitHub's existing platform, for you to review at your leasure;
